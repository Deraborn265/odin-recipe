SHRIMP MARINARA

Description

This dish serves a lot of nutrition advantages some of them are

  • Helps in the formation of red blood cels
  • Protect cell damage
  • acquiring of vitamin B12

The dish has it origins from Southeast Asia

Ingredients

  • 1 can Italian diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoon minced fresh parsely
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon papper
  • 1 pound uncooked shrimp (26-30 per pound), peeled and deveined
  • 3/4 pound spaghetti, cooked and drained
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese, optional

Steps

  1. In a 3-qt. slow cooker, combine the first 9 ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 3-4 hours.
  2. Stir in shrimp. Cover and cook on high 15-25 minutes, just until shrimp turn pink. Serve with spaghetti. Sprinkle with cheese if desired.
